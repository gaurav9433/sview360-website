SInsight AI

AI-Assisted Video Analytics for Faster Operator Response

SInsight adds intelligent event detection to SView360, helping operators identify important events faster. Analytics are designed to support human verification, not replace security teams or certified life-safety systems.

  • Fire & Smoke Pattern Detection
  • After-Hours Movement
  • Loitering Detection
  • Camera Tamper Detection
  • People Counting
  • Crowd Monitoring
  • Line Crossing
  • Restricted Area Entry
  • ATM Area Monitoring
  • PPE / Safety Compliance

AI-assisted analytics are intended for operator verification and situational awareness. Fire and smoke detection analytics are not a replacement for certified fire alarm systems or life-safety infrastructure.
