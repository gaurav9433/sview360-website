FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

  • What is the minimum deployment size?
    Minimum is 50 cameras with a one-time platform license. There is no minimum contract duration — cameras can be added or removed monthly.
  • Can we use SView360 without replacing our existing cameras?
    Yes. SView360 is designed to work with existing ONVIF-compatible IP cameras and NVRs from brands including Hikvision, Dahua, CP Plus, Axis, Uniview, Bosch, and others. Contact us to verify support for your specific camera models.
  • What is the difference between cloud and hybrid deployment?
    In full cloud deployment, SView360 manages live streaming and video storage entirely in the cloud — no local NVR required. In hybrid deployment, your existing NVR handles primary video storage while SView360 adds cloud visibility, alarm management, compliance reporting, and AI analytics on top. Hybrid pricing is lower because you absorb primary storage costs.
  • Do you offer a free trial?
    Yes. Contact sales@seple.in to arrange a 30-day pilot for your environment. We will work with your team to configure a representative deployment so you can evaluate the platform on real cameras and sites.
  • Where is our video data stored?
    Cloud and hybrid deployments use AWS infrastructure in the Mumbai region (ap-south-1). Video and metadata do not leave India. On-premise deployments store all data within your own infrastructure.
  • What compliance standards does SView360 support?
    SView360 is designed with compliance-oriented recording visibility, audit logging, evidence workflows, and role-based access control. Contact our team to discuss alignment with your specific regulatory requirements.
  • How long does implementation take?
    Typical implementations for 50–200 cameras take 2–4 weeks including configuration, hierarchy setup, user training, and go-live support. Larger deployments are scoped individually.
  • Is there a discount for security integrators?
    Yes. Integrators managing 50 or more cameras under a partner account qualify for volume pricing and access to partner billing tools. Contact sales@seple.in to apply for the integrator program.
  • Can SView360 be deployed on-premise or in an air-gapped environment?
    Yes. Perpetual licensing is available for environments requiring private infrastructure, restricted networks, or complete data sovereignty. Contact us for a project-specific quotation.
  • What AI analytics modules are available?
    Available modules include fire and smoke detection, after-hours movement, loitering, camera tamper, people counting, crowd density monitoring, line crossing, restricted area entry, and PPE compliance detection. AI analytics are licensed per camera where the module is active.
  • What happens if a camera goes offline?
    SView360 monitors camera health in real time and generates alerts for offline cameras, recording gaps, and stream quality issues. Exception reports can be scheduled for regular review.
  • How is billing handled for integrators managing multiple customers?
    Integrator accounts include partner billing tools to manage separate invoicing per customer or consolidated billing. White-label branding and custom domain options are available. Contact sales@seple.in for partner program details.

Hybrid Deployment — Local Recording + Cloud Intelligence

In hybrid deployment, your cameras continue recording locally to your existing NVR infrastructure — so you keep your hardware investment and your primary video storage stays on-site. SView360 connects via the SLink edge gateway and adds centralized cloud visibility, alarm verification, compliance reporting, evidence management, and AI analytics on top.

Because your NVR handles primary video storage, your monthly cloud fee is lower than a full cloud deployment — you are only paying for what SView360 provides in the cloud, not for replacing infrastructure you already own.
